Craving

Echoes of the Veil Book 2

The city is cracking under pressure: political decay, rising seas, and a string of brutal murders that make the powers that be uncomfortable. Surveillance is constant, truth is malleable, and justice serves only the worthy—or so they claim.

As the investigation deepens, facts blur into fiction. Everyone has something to hide. Everyone is a suspect.

Told with sharp prose and a slow-burning sense of dread, Craving is a speculative thriller about desire, control, and the dark corners we keep hidden—even from ourselves.

Author: C.E. Michel
