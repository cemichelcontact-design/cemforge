Echoes of the Veil

The Priest, the Devil, and the Angel

The world is fractured. The prophecy is forbidden. The divine is no longer silent.

In a land still trembling from ancient wars, a priest, a devil, and an angel are drawn together by whispers of something long buried. As memory and truth begin to unravel, their paths reveal a world older, darker, and far more dangerous than anyone imagined.

Echoes of the Veil is a mythic dark fantasy about fate, forbidden power, and what lies between salvation and ruin.

Author: C.E. Michel
